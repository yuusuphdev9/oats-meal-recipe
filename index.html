OAT MEAL RECIPE

This is my go-to healthy oatmeal recipe. It's creamy, nourishing, and delicious with delicious toppings! Find a few of my favorite combinations below.


Prep Time:

5-10 minutes

Cook Time:

10 minutes

Servings:

Better served with Toppings


INGREDIENTS;

  • 1 cup water
  • Â½ cup whole rolled oats
  • Sugar or Salt
  • Milk
  • Honey or Maple Syrup, optional
  • Frozen blueberries, optional
  • Coconut Flakes, optional
  • Banana, optional

INSTRUCTIONS;

    1. In a small pot, stir together the water, oats, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low
    2. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 2 to 5 minutes, or until the oatmeal is thick and creamy
    3. Stir in a pinch of sugar or salt according to preffered taste
    4. Serve hot with honey or maple syrup, if desired
    5. Add milk to desired taste
    6. Top with blueberries,diced banana and coconut flakes, if desired

TIPS;
  • Get the ratio right
  • Swap water for milk
  • Stir, stir, stir
  • Don't overcook the oats
Nutrition Facts;
  • Calories: ~154 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 27.4 g
  • Protein: 6 g
  • Fat: 3.2 g
